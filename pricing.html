Pricing FAQ

Common questions before enrollment

Is the price really $10 per hour?

Yes. One full hour is $10 USD. A 30-minute class is $5, and a 45-minute class is $7.50. Monthly pricing is calculated using a simple 4-week month.

Which class length is best for children?

Many younger children do well with 30 minutes, especially at the beginning. Older children and adults often benefit from 45 minutes or 1 hour, depending on focus and goals.

Can I study Quran, Tajweed, Hifz, Arabic, or Islamic Studies with the same pricing?

Yes. The pricing page is based on class length and weekly frequency, not only one subject. You can discuss the exact course goal on WhatsApp before starting.

How do I enroll?

Click any Enroll button and send the automatic WhatsApp message. We will ask for the student level, age, preferred time, class length, and number of days per week.

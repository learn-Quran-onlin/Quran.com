Overview

About this course

Private Quran classes for adults who want to improve reading, Tajweed, memorization, or Quranic Arabic with a respectful pace and flexible timing.

The lesson is kept simple, clear, and practical. The teacher begins by checking the learner’s current level, then builds a weekly plan with revision, new material, live correction, and homework that the learner can actually maintain.

Learning Objectives

What you will learn

  • Start from your actual level without embarrassment
  • Correct long-standing pronunciation mistakes
  • Build a realistic practice routine around work and family
  • Learn Tajweed gradually while reciting
  • Choose reading, memorization, Arabic, or mixed goals
Ideal Learners

This course is ideal for

  • Adults starting later in life
  • Busy professionals
  • Reverts and non-Arabic speakers
  • Students who want private correction
Course Features

Simple, structured, and parent-friendly

Level assessment

The teacher checks reading, pronunciation, speed, confidence, and goals before starting.

Private attention

Every class focuses on one learner, so mistakes are corrected immediately.

Clear lesson path

Students know what they are learning now, what comes next, and what to revise.

Progress updates

Parents or adult learners receive simple feedback and next steps.

Expected Outcomes

By the end of this course, the learner should be able to

  • feel confident reading aloud
  • understand your mistakes clearly
  • keep a sustainable weekly routine
  • move steadily toward your Quran goals
